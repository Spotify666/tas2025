How to book a slot

Choose your sport, timing and duration. Confirm via your preferred channel — online form, app, or direct contact.

  • Tap on the date to open the booking form and fill in your details.
  • Complete payment as per the instructions (UPI / bank / on-venue).
  • Receive confirmation with slot ID and arrive 10–15 minutes early.
